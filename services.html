Tile Roofing — Clay & Concrete

Tile roofs are the signature look of many Florida communities — including the planned neighborhoods of Wesley Chapel and Land O Lakes. When properly installed and maintained, clay and concrete tile roofs can last 50+ years and withstand Florida's harshest weather conditions.

Flat & Low-Slope Roof Repair

Many Florida homes have flat or low-pitch roof sections — over garages, additions, lanais, and covered entries. These sections require different roofing systems than sloped roofs, and they need contractors who understand the unique challenges of low-slope drainage and waterproofing in Florida's heavy rain environment.

We install and repair TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen flat roofing systems that are proven performers in the Florida climate. If you have a flat roof section that leaks or has aged out, we can assess whether a coating/repair or full replacement is the right call.

  • TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) installation and repair
  • EPDM rubber roofing systems
  • Modified bitumen installation and repair
  • Reflective roof coatings
  • Drain and scupper repair/installation
  • Ponding water correction

Gutter Installation & Repair

Florida receives more annual rainfall than almost anywhere in the continental US — and without properly sized gutters, all that water collects around your foundation, erodes landscaping, and can cause serious structural problems over time. Gutters are not optional in Florida; they're essential infrastructure.

We install seamless aluminum gutters custom-fabricated on-site to fit your home perfectly. Seamless gutters have no joints (except at corners and downspouts), which dramatically reduces leak points. We size everything properly for Florida's heavy rainfall events — not just the light stuff.

  • Seamless aluminum gutters in 20+ colors
  • 5" and 6" gutter profiles available
  • Downspout extensions and underground drainage
  • Gutter guards and leaf protection systems
  • Gutter cleaning and maintenance
