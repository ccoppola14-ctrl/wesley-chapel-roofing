Our Story

Built in Wesley Chapel. Rooted in Pasco County.

Wesley Chapel Roofing Pro was founded by people who grew up watching this area transform from open land into one of the fastest-growing communities in Florida. We've watched neighborhoods like Wiregrass Ranch, Seven Oaks, Meadow Pointe, and Epperson develop — and we've reroofed homes in all of them.

When we started, we made a simple promise: treat every homeowner the way we'd want someone to treat our own family. Give honest assessments. Charge fair prices. Do the work right. Show up when we say we will. It sounds basic, but in the contracting world, it's rarer than it should be.

Over the years, that promise built a reputation. Today, more than half our new customers come from referrals — neighbors telling neighbors, real estate agents recommending us to buyers, property managers calling us first when something goes wrong. That's the kind of business we're proud of.

We've been through multiple hurricane seasons, helped homeowners navigate increasingly complex insurance claims, and watched Florida's roofing codes evolve. We've adapted, invested in training, and earned certifications to stay at the front of our industry. And we've never stopped being the guys you can actually call.
