Questions Roofers Ask

Are these leads shared with other roofing companies?

No. Every lead goes to ONE roofer per zip code. If you're in Wesley Chapel, a Wesley Chapel roofing lead goes to you and only you.

What counts as a "lead"?

A lead is either: (1) an inbound phone call from a homeowner in your territory, or (2) a form submission with name, phone, and description of the roofing issue — including insurance claims.

Do you get storm damage / insurance leads?

Yes. "Storm damage roof repair" and "hail damage roofer" searches are some of the highest-intent searches in our markets. These often turn into $20,000+ insurance jobs.

How do you generate these leads?

Local SEO + Google Business Profile optimization + targeted content. Homeowners searching "roofing contractor near me" or "[city] roof repair" find our pages first — and we route them to you.

What if I'm already working with HomeAdvisor or Angi?

These platforms shared leads with multiple contractors and homeowners are comparison shopping. Our leads are exclusive, local, and higher-intent. Stack them — don't replace.

What's the minimum commitment?

No long-term contracts. Start with 5 trial leads at $125 each to prove the quality. If it works, we can set up a monthly package (typically 10–15 leads/month).
